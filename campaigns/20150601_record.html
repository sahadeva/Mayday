Citizen Recording Studio

Record a message that we'll deliver to key Congress members' offices. Here are a few helpful tips, but remember: If you're unhappy with your first try, you can always re-record your message until you are happy.

  1. Say your name, phone number, and that you're a voter.
    Remember: Be confident, speak slowly, and don't forget to breathe.
  2. Explain that you're asking the senator to support S. 1538, The Fair Elections Now Act.
  3. Thank the Senator for listening to citizens instead of special interests.
  4. When you finish your message, stay on the line to review or re-record your message.
